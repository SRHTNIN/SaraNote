Chaotic neutral

Powers: Immortality

Their entire body lacks skin, and it disturbs most. They look similar to Curly from mouthwashing.

A person with the power of immortality. They cannot die, and always regenerate. However, their power only regenerates the bare minimum. Bone, blood, and muscle.A lot of people would presume this person to be evil. They rarely speak, and nobody knows much of them. While they are the ultimate defense, they are quite weak, only relying on human muscles and strength. Due to their long lifetime and immunity to pain, they often rip off parts of their body to attack. To escape or be mobile, they rip off their own head and throw it. As their headless body collapses, a new one grows from their severed head.
