An old man who's nearly always seen wearing his knight armour, donned with the crest of Greibrixun and with a dark grey accent colour.

An old knight who still serves his city. He was deemed a Sir by the grey kings themselves. He is usually cheery and calm, though he can immediately become a true danger if he sees someone hurting someone else, even outside of his city. He's stationed at the entrances of the city, so many travellers know of and like him.
