#Eerie #Roguelike #Horror #Management

No Signal at Terminus
Dimension: 3D
Viewpoint: First person
Mechanic: Conducting a train and having to manage resources and customers.
Main Character: The Conductress

Gameplay Loop:
Upgrade train and get customers at station -> Drive to correct stops -> Explore -> Survive threats ->/

Description:
You, the conductress, have been assigned to drive BT07 so that all passengers, no matter who they are, may arrive on schedule.
